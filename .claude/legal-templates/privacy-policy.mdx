**Financial Information:**

## 7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

### 7.1 What We Use

We use the following technologies to collect NPII:

- **Cookies:** Small text files stored on your device that help us recognize you and remember your preferences
- **Web beacons/pixel tags:** Small images embedded in web pages or emails that track user activity
- **Log files:** Server records of your activity on our Website

### 7.2 Types of Cookies

- **Session cookies:** Temporary cookies deleted when you close your browser
- **Persistent cookies:** Remain on your device until they expire or you delete them
- **Third-party cookies:** Placed by our advertising and analytics partners

### 7.3 Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings:
- Block all cookies
- Accept only first-party cookies
- Clear existing cookies

Note that disabling cookies may affect Website functionality.

### 7.4 Do Not Track

We do not currently respond to "Do Not Track" browser signals. However, you can opt out of certain tracking as described above.
