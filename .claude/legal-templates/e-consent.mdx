# Consent for Electronic Signatures, Records, and Disclosures (E-Consent)

**Effective Date:** {props.config.effectiveDate}

**Last Updated:** {props.config.lastUpdated}

This E-Consent disclosure ("E-Consent") applies to all communications between you and {props.config.domain} ("Website," "we," "us," "our") and to communications with third-party lenders and service providers ("Service Providers") you may be connected with through our Website.

**Please read this E-Consent carefully before submitting any information on our Website.**

---

## 1. Your Consent

By submitting your information through our Website, you:

1. **Consent to electronic communications:** You agree to receive all communications, documents, disclosures, and notices from us and our Service Providers electronically rather than in paper form.

2. **Consent to electronic signatures:** You agree that your electronic signature has the same legal effect as a handwritten signature.

3. **Consent to electronic records:** You agree that electronic records satisfy any requirement that documents be "in writing."

4. **Acknowledge E-SIGN Act applicability:** You acknowledge this consent is provided pursuant to the Electronic Signatures in Global and National Commerce Act (E-SIGN Act), 15 U.S.C. ยง 7001 et seq.

---

## 2. Scope of Communications Covered

This E-Consent applies to all electronic communications related to:

**From Us:**
- Confirmation of your loan request submission
- Status updates on your request
- Privacy notices and disclosures
- Changes to our terms and policies
- Marketing and promotional communications (where you have consented)
- Responses to your inquiries

**From Service Providers (once connected):**
- Loan applications and agreements
- Truth in Lending disclosures
- Privacy notices
- Account statements and payment information
- Due date reminders
- Collection notices
- Any legally required disclosures

---

## 3. Methods of Electronic Delivery

Electronic communications may be delivered by:

- **Email:** To the email address you provide
- **Website posting:** Through your account portal or on our Website
- **SMS/Text message:** To the mobile number you provide
- **In-app notifications:** If accessing through a mobile application
- **Downloadable documents:** PDF or other electronic document formats

---

## 4. Hardware and Software Requirements

To receive, access, and retain electronic communications, you need:

**Hardware:**
- A computer, smartphone, or tablet with internet access
- Sufficient storage space to save or download documents

**Software:**
- A current, supported web browser:
  - Google Chrome (current version)
  - Mozilla Firefox (current version)
  - Apple Safari (current version)
  - Microsoft Edge (current version)
- A PDF reader (such as Adobe Acrobat Reader) for certain documents
- An active email account capable of receiving emails with attachments

**Connectivity:**
- A reliable internet connection

**If these requirements change, we will notify you and provide updated requirements.**

---

## 5. How to Obtain Paper Copies

You have the right to receive paper copies of any electronic communication. To request a paper copy:

**From Us:**
- Email: {props.config.supportEmail}
- We will mail a paper copy at no charge

**From Service Providers:**
- Contact the Service Provider directly using the contact information in their communications
- Service Provider policies on paper copies may vary

---

## 6. Withdrawing Your Consent

### 6.1 How to Withdraw

You may withdraw your consent to receive electronic communications at any time by:
- Emailing us at {props.config.supportEmail}
- Contacting Service Providers directly regarding their communications

### 6.2 Effect of Withdrawal

**Withdrawal from Us:** If you withdraw consent before being connected with a Service Provider, we may be unable to process your request.

**Withdrawal from Service Providers:** Once connected with a Service Provider, your withdrawal must be submitted directly to them. Withdrawal may limit their ability to service your account. The Service Provider's policies will govern the effect of withdrawal.

### 6.3 Prior Communications

Withdrawal of consent does not affect the legal validity or enforceability of:
- Communications delivered before your withdrawal
- Electronic signatures you provided before withdrawal
- Agreements entered into electronically before withdrawal

## 7. Updating Your Contact Information

**You are responsible for keeping your contact information current.**

If your email address, phone number, or mailing address changes, you must:
1. Update your information with us by emailing {props.config.supportEmail}
2. Update your information with any Service Provider you have been connected with

Failure to maintain current contact information may result in missed communications, which could affect your loan application or account.

## 8. Communications Are Legally Binding

All electronic communications provided under this E-Consent:
- Constitute "writings" under applicable law
- Have the same legal effect as paper documents
- Are admissible as evidence in legal proceedings
- Satisfy any requirement for written notice or disclosure

**Our Retention:**
We retain electronic records in accordance with our records retention policies and applicable law. We are not required to maintain copies indefinitely.

**Your Responsibility:**
We recommend you:
- Save or print copies of all important communications
- Maintain your own records of electronic agreements and disclosures
- Download any documents you may need before account closure

**We are not responsible for Service Provider communications or their compliance with electronic disclosure requirements.**
