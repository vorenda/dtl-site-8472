## California Residents: Your CCPA/CPRA Rights

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA).

### Your Rights

**1. Right to Know**
You have the right to request that we disclose:
- The categories of personal information we collected about you
- The categories of sources from which we collected your personal information
- Our business or commercial purpose for collecting, selling, or sharing your personal information
- The categories of third parties with whom we share your personal information
- The specific pieces of personal information we collected about you

**2. Right to Delete**
You have the right to request that we delete personal information we collected from you, subject to certain exceptions allowed by law.

**3. Right to Correct**
You have the right to request correction of inaccurate personal information we maintain about you.

**4. Right to Opt-Out of Sale or Sharing**
You have the right to direct us not to "sell" or "share" your personal information. Under California law:
- "Sale" includes disclosing personal information for monetary or other valuable consideration
- "Sharing" includes disclosing personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising

**We may sell or share your personal information with third-party lenders and marketing partners as described in our Privacy Policy.**

**5. Right to Limit Use of Sensitive Personal Information**
You have the right to limit our use of your sensitive personal information (such as Social Security Number, financial account information) to uses necessary to provide the services you request.

**6. Right to Non-Discrimination**
We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.

### Categories of Personal Information We Collect

| Category | Examples | Collected | Disclosed to Third Parties |
|----------|----------|-----------|---------------------------|
| Identifiers | Name, address, email, phone, SSN, IP address | Yes | Yes |
| Financial Information | Bank account, income, employment info | Yes | Yes |
| Commercial Information | Loan requests, products/services considered | Yes | Yes |
| Internet Activity | Browsing history, search history on our site | Yes | Yes |
| Geolocation | Approximate location from IP address | Yes | Yes |
| Inferences | Profiles reflecting preferences, creditworthiness | Yes | Yes |
| Sensitive Personal Information | SSN, driver's license, financial accounts | Yes | Yes |

### Categories of Third Parties

We disclose personal information to:
- Third-party lenders and financial service providers
- Marketing and advertising partners
- Data analytics providers
- Service providers (IT, security, customer service)

### Sources of Personal Information

We collect personal information from:
- You, when you submit a loan request form
- Your devices, automatically through cookies and tracking technologies
- Third-party data providers (for identity verification)

### Business Purposes for Collection

We collect and use personal information to:
- Connect you with lenders who may offer loan products
- Process and route your loan requests
- Verify your identity and prevent fraud
- Communicate with you about your request
- Improve our Website and services
- Comply with legal obligations

### Retention Period

We retain personal information as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in our Privacy Policy, typically:
- Active request data: Duration of request processing plus 7 years
- Marketing data: Until you opt out or request deletion
- Analytics data: 26 months

## Virginia Residents: Your VCDPA Rights

If you are a Virginia resident, you have specific rights under the Virginia Consumer Data Protection Act (VCDPA), effective January 1, 2023.

### Your Rights

**1. Right to Access**
You have the right to confirm whether we process your personal data and to access that data.

**2. Right to Correct**
You have the right to correct inaccuracies in your personal data.

**3. Right to Delete**
You have the right to delete personal data you provided to us or that we obtained about you.

**4. Right to Data Portability**
You have the right to obtain a copy of your personal data in a portable, readily usable format.

**5. Right to Opt Out**
You have the right to opt out of:
- Processing for targeted advertising
- Sale of personal data
- Profiling in furtherance of decisions that produce legal or similarly significant effects

### Sensitive Data

We process sensitive data (including Social Security Number and financial information) only with your consent, which you provide when you submit a loan request.

### Appeals

If we decline your request, you may appeal by emailing {props.config.supportEmail}. We will respond to appeals within 60 days. If we deny your appeal, we will explain our reasoning. You may then contact the Virginia Attorney General at https://www.oag.state.va.us/consumer-protection/index.php/file-a-complaint.

## How to Submit a Privacy Request

### Methods to Submit

**Email:** {props.config.supportEmail}

**Online Form:** Complete the form below

### Verification Process

To protect your privacy, we must verify your identity before processing your request. We may ask you to:
- Confirm information we have on file (name, email, phone, address)
- Provide additional identifying information
- Respond to a verification email or text

For sensitive requests (such as specific pieces of personal information), we may require additional verification steps.

### Authorized Agents

You may designate an authorized agent to submit requests on your behalf. We may require:
- Written authorization signed by you
- Verification of the agent's identity
- Direct verification from you that you authorized the agent

### Response Timeline

- **California:** We will respond within 45 days. We may extend this by an additional 45 days if reasonably necessary (we will notify you of any extension).
- **Virginia:** We will respond within 45 days. We may extend this by an additional 45 days if reasonably necessary.
